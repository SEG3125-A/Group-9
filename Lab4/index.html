Meet Our Team

Welcome to Bicycool, our team of passionate and highly skilled individuals, each dedicated to providing top-notch bike repair services. With a deep love for all things cycling, we take pride in delivering exceptional craftsmanship and unparalleled expertise to every bike that comes through our workshop doors.

Whether it's optimizing brakes, repairing puncrues, or delivering a full maintenance, we deliver each service with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that every bike leaving our workshop is elevated to peak performance.

Meet the faces behind the expertise:

Leo Cole

Leo Cole

With over 15 years of experience, Leo is our go-to expert for diagnosing and fixing complex bike issues. His passion for cycling and meticulous approach ensures every repair meets the highest standards.

Kate Phillips

Kate Phillips

Kate ensures a seamless customer experience from drop-off to pick-up. Her friendly demeanor and knowledge of bike repairs make her the perfect point of contact for all customer inquiries and service updates.

Lynn Myers
